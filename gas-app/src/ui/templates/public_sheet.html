Rebuild Photos_NNN folders / 重建 Photos_NNN 文件夹

For every event, regenerate the flat <Event>/Photos_001/, Photos_002/, … shortcut buckets that consolidate every uploaded photo under the event. Use after manual Drive edits or when an older event is missing its buckets. May take a few minutes per event for large archives — refreshes the public sheet at the end automatically.
为每个活动重建 Photos_001/002/… 快捷方式文件夹；可在 Drive 手动调整后补齐。完成后自动刷新公开表格。

videocam

Rebuild Videos folders / 重建 Videos 文件夹

For every (event, club, tag) upload-link triple, regenerate the <Event>/<Club>/<Tag>/Videos/ shortcut folder that indexes every video under that scope. Idempotent — folders that already exist are updated in place. Refreshes the public sheet at the end automatically.
为每个（活动、跑团、标签）三元组重建 Videos 快捷方式文件夹，幂等更新。完成后自动刷新公开表格。

Ready
Don't usually need to click these. The public sheet auto-refreshes after every successful upload, and a scheduled trigger re-runs the refresh every 30 minutes as a safety net. Use these buttons only when you've made a manual change in Drive and want the public view to catch up immediately.
通常无需手动刷新 — 每次上传成功后会自动刷新，并有 30 分钟一次的定时任务兜底。仅在手动修改 Drive 后需要时使用。
