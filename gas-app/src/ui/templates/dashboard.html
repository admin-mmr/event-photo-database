How to upload photos / 如何上传照片
Photos are submitted using an Upload Link — a unique URL tied to a specific event and running club. Admins generate links from the Links page and share them with photographers. Anyone who receives a link can upload photos directly, without needing an account in this system.
照片通过上传链接（Upload Link）提交。管理员在 Links 页面为每场活动和跑团生成专属链接， 再将链接发给摄影志愿者。持有链接的人可直接上传照片，无需系统账号。
